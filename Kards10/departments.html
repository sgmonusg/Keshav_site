Departments


Department of Sponsorship and Marketing

It’s probably the most important department on campus, at least in regards to fests. After all, everything you see in a fest is possible because DoSM brings in the sponsorship for these fests from different companies. If it weren’t for them, there would be no money to fund the fests. This department consists of students dedicated to our college who work hard throughout the year, contacting hundreds of companies and do the marketing of our fests. The responsibility of ensuring that the SU has enough to funds for the smooth functioning of the fests is completely upon them.

The sponsorship, either in cash or in kind, is brought in different ways. The most common process is to contact a company on phone and pitch the fest to them. Another, more tedious path that they often have to take is when they have to visit the corporate office of the companies in the city and talk to the concerned person regarding our fest. Their job doesn’t just end at bringing in the money. They have to ensure that the promises made to the companies are fulfilled completely, to maintain a healthy working relationship with them. Doing so, also makes it easier to call them for future fests. This part includes the putting up of huge banners, posters, dropdowns and sometimes balloons; all throughout the campus during the fest, so that the promised publicity of the companies is done.

The members of this department work tirelessly throughout the duration of the fest, sacrificing their enjoyment, handing out coupons to the students of the entire campus to ensure that the various stalls function in an organised manner. The work goes on for a few weeks after the completion of the fest as they to have collect all the data, prepare a branding report for each company and send it to them to assure them of the work done for them on fest. Apart from all this, they are the only department which literally works all year around, even in the vacations. During the summer break, they work for ATMOS; in winter, for Pearl and ARENA. They connect BITS to the professional world, talking to the executives of various companies. They also bring in separate sponsorships for Corporate Social Responsibility

Department of Publicity and Public relations

The Department of Publicity and Public Relations works towards increasing awareness about the campus and its multitude of fests among the masses that lie beyond our four walls. DePP is one of the few departments that are active throughout the year (in the form of online publicity). All the endeavours that make a fest - gathering sponsorship, preparing for events and workshops, etc. - ultimately boil down to a singular metric in the eyes of the public. That metric is footfall, and we at DePP can proudly claim to be the reason for a tremendous majority of it. Whether it be centralising the flow of posters, managing of the social media presence of the fests, or making trips to other colleges in person, DePP is responsible for it all. Due to the efforts of our many qualified members, footfall, awareness and online presence have all been increasing exponentially every passing year.

Department of Security and Hospitality

DoSH or the Department of Security and Hospitality is responsible for channelling all the excitement of the tonnes of participants, plethora of events, variety of proshows that’s part and parcel of every BPHC fest there is. From the first step of issuing the registration cards for the outside participants, to providing them with the accommodation facilities in the hostels and ensuring diligent crowd management for the proshows, every member of Department of Security and Hospitality works with utmost regard and hard work towards making the fest a worthwhile experience for everyone involved. Being part of DoSH instils a pride in you just as anyone would feel in joining the League of Superheroes.

Department of Technical Arts

Department of Technical Arts, commonly shortened as DoTA is a collection of like-minded technically oriented members looking to hone their skills and put it to use in service of the college fests. The responsibilities of DoTA include Web and Graphic design, web development and app development. The Graphic designers of DoTA members take up the responsibility of making awe-inspiring creative posters to put out for our fests, thus making DoTA central to the publicity of our fests. Apart from Graphic design, under Web development DoTA goes out of it’s way to make sure all the fest’s online presences and landing pages look and function way beyond the level of ordinary college websites
