Clubs


Dance Club

The Dance Club comprises of a group of students who have one thing in common: their passion and love for dance. Their innovative performances incorporate various dance styles and techniques, whether it be a simple Western dance routine or even something involving the application of technology. The club conducts occasional workshops and hosts exciting events during Pearl, the Cultural Fest.

Music Club

We're a close knit group of versatile people who like making cool music and doing exactly that is at the core of all our identities. We love music, and we love involving as many people as is possible in this process.

What we convey through music, is far more than what we'd be capable of with words.

Movie Club

Movies are the greatest form of knowledge and entertainment. We learn so much from them. Some Movies inspire us, some move our soul, some show us the reality, some make us live our greatest desires. The Movie Club, BITS Hyderabad, recognizes this, and aims solely at developing the movie culture at BPHC. We have been involved in film making since our very inception. We have been very keen on writing original scripts and producing short films on campus. We have produced about 25 videos in the past three years. Also, we organize slumber parties, the all night Movie screenings during various fests throughout the year. And these slumber parties have become synonymous to the college and are the greatest attractions of any fest. This year “The Movie Thursdays” initiative was taken up by us, wherein we show various critically acclaimed movies every Thursday, including foreign movies, which have great content and value. We have successfully organized 2 editions of Catharsis Film Festival. Our motive is to entertain BPHC, and in the process, make people here familiar with the rich and beautiful world of cinema.

VFx Club

The VFx Club of BPHC is one of the most active clubs on campus and takes care of the visual media needs of the campus. The VFx Club oversees independent creative projects that are visually extravagant and various other club collaboration projects. It aims at improving the video editing and visual effects skill set of the student body. In a world where visual media without added effects is rare, we strive to make the requisite skill set less intimidating and more approachable. Our vision is to impart graphic designing skills to as many students as possible and encourage innovation. We believe in learning by practice and encourage the club members and enthusiasts to create something new by utilizing the imparted skills.

English Language Activities Society

Pensive poets to incessant blabbermouths to obsessive grammar Nazis - we are the eccentric lovers of the language, united in our pursuit of literary activities.The English Language Activities Society (ELAS) aims to promote among its members and in the college community a larger appreciation of literature and the arts, and social intercourse founded upon such appreciation.

The club is responsible for all literary activities on campus. With a wide array of events ranging from Words of a feather, Picture Perspective, Flash Fiction to JAM, Extempore, Block ‘N Tackle and debates, the club strives to fulfill this mandate.The club also organizes a plethora of other events, both formal and otherwise, which include round-robin stories with multiple authors, groups for writing and discussions on books and scrabble nights.The club also organizes Verba Maximus, the national literary fest of BITS Pilani Hyderabad.

ELAS Blog coming soon.

Photog Club

The Photography Club (Photog) of BPHC is amongst the most active and enthusiastic clubs on campus and caters to the coverage needs of various events. Photog has numerous events of its own ranging from Photog Race to Photo-Booths to Treasure Hunts. Photog is also responsible for holding workshops, seminars and talks by inviting successful photographers to help impart a better photography culture on campus. It aims at improving both the clicking and post-processing skill set of the students. Even when not being a performing club, Photog has made its mark in all assets and has given the learners a totally new perspective of seeing the world. Our goal is to impart the Photography skills to as many people as possible to bring out the artist in them. We believe in learning by experience and encourage the Club members and enthusiasts to utilize the creativity inside them coupled with the knowledge we provide them with to capture extraordinary moments.

Quiz Club

The Quiz Club, BITS Pilani Hyderabad or as fondly referred by people as Sorcerers of Intellect is one of the most active clubs on campus with Quizzes happening every week throughout the semester. Club's activities include conducting Quizzes for people on campus and during fests, as well as provide integrated quizzing solutions to all the other clubs and associations on campus. We also conduct a National Level General Quiz by the name ,ENIGMA ,as a part of the technical fest ATMOS, held in various cities across India seeing participation from more than 300 colleges. We have also hosted reputed Quizmasters like Chandrakant Nair, Navin Rajaram, Venkatesh Srinivasan and Vikram Joshi to name a few and have been an integral part of the Hyderabad Quizzing scene since inception in 2008. All in all, if you are even remotely interested in anything , this is the place to be, as we have enthusiasts on every topic of human knowledge.

Happy Quizzing!

Bulls and Bears Club

The BULLS and BEARS club is the Finance and Investment Club on campus and works towards a goal of "financial literacy". The club organizes games and events to ease down the tonic of overly complicated Wall Street jargon. We deal with the concepts of equity investments, financial institutions, monetary mechanisms, valuation principals, and all things that make money.

Journal Club

The Journal club of BPHC , the most happening club on campus is responsible for undertaking almost all of the journalism activities on campus from providing content to the websites to the making of the fest magazines, also taking care of the video journalism. We at JC also look into investigative journalism, release monthly newsletters and manymore. We hold events, workshops( Wikipedia editing) and more to keep the journalism culture going. With the most enthusiastic members Journal Club is one huge family looking at growing and improving in what they do every year. Sounds interesting? Come be a part of our family!

Hindi Tarang

Can you feel the words?Can you weave emotions in your words? If yes,you fight right in. We , at Hindi Tarang,do exactly the same. Let all the emotions ,thoughts take the form of words expressed in the language you are most comfortable with,Hindi. Designers Anonymous

The design club of the campus, Designers Anonymous, is a congregation of people who have a passion for graphic design and the digital arts. It came into existence only last year although the work produced is of high quality. The club members make use of softwares like Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator to create artworks throughout the year, unlike DoTA, where the graphic design team works specifically for fests. The aim of the club is to create a blooming design culture among the campus students.

Martial Arts Club

When we involve in a fight, the greatest obstacle we face is our own fear .Martial arts teach us not to react to other’s problems, but to pay attention to our own. We believe that the world gets better and the problems get smaller when one develops self confidence in oneself, which learning martial arts does. With unmatched integrity and energy, as a group, we mutually help each other develop our martial arts skill and focus mainly on mixed type of martial arts.

As martial arts are about utilizing skill rather than brute force, it is open and accessible to all. Our club opens its doors to anyone who is looking to improve flexibility, fitness, balance and combative skills .

The Daily Bitsian

The Daily Bitsian is the online campus magazine of BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus. We release an issue every month. We like to call ourselves a Creative Corporation because the work we do has no borders when it comes to the medium we express in, because all you need is an idea which you channel it through any medium, to be a part of our community. We also act as the Curators of all artists on campus. The community that we're building is a place where writers, designers, photographers, and anyone with a story to tell, gets the space and means to do so.

We're constantly looking for your contributions with the intent to truly be able to represent what the campus stands for. Our work is aggregated at thedailybitsian.wordpress.com
